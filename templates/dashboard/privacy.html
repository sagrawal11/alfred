Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and handled for this personal SMS-based assistant operated by Sarthak Agrawal.

This service is intended for personal use by a single user (the owner) and is not offered to the general public.

Information Collected

The service may collect and process the following information:

  • Phone number used to send and receive SMS messages
  • Message content voluntarily sent by the user (e.g., reminders, notes, logs)
  • Message metadata required to deliver SMS messages (such as timestamps)

No additional personal information is collected beyond what is necessary to operate SMS messaging.

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to:

  • Send and receive SMS messages
  • Provide reminders, notifications, and assistant responses
  • Maintain basic message history for functionality and continuity

Messages are primarily user-initiated and informational in nature.

Data Sharing

Message data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Information may be processed by third-party service providers only as required to deliver SMS messages, such as messaging and telecommunications providers (e.g., Twilio).

Data Retention

Message data is retained only as long as necessary to operate the service and may be deleted at any time by the owner.

SMS Consent

By sending hi alfred (or START) to the associated Toll-Free number, the user explicitly consents to receive SMS text messages from Sarthak Agrawal.

Consent is not shared with any other parties and is limited to this service.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect message data and prevent unauthorized access. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or SMS messaging, reply HELP to any message from this service.
