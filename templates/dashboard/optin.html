SMS Opt-In & Messaging Disclosure

Business Name: Sarthak Agrawal

Service Type: Personal SMS Assistant (Single User)

SMS Opt-In

This service uses SMS text messaging to provide a personal assistant experience.

By opting in, you agree to receive text messages from Sarthak Agrawal.

How to Opt In

To opt in, send the following keyword via SMS to the Toll-Free phone number associated with this service:

START

By sending START, you explicitly consent to receive SMS text messages from Sarthak Agrawal.

No messages are sent unless and until you complete this opt-in step.

Opt-In Confirmation Message

After opting in, you will receive the following confirmation message:

"You are now opted in to receive text messages from Sarthak Agrawal. You will receive SMS messages related to events, account notifications, and higher education updates, including personal reminders, tracking entries, and assistant responses. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for more information."

Types of Messages You May Receive

After opting in, you may receive SMS messages including:

  • Account notifications (confirmations, summaries, reminders)
  • Event-related messages (scheduled reminders, deadlines)
  • Higher education–related messages (assignments, academic planning)
  • Responses from a personal SMS-based assistant based on messages you send

Messages are informational and user-initiated in nature.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out at any time by replying:

STOP

After opting out, you will receive the following confirmation:

"You have been unsubscribed from messages from Sarthak Agrawal. You will no longer receive SMS messages. Reply START to re-subscribe."

No further messages will be sent unless you opt back in.

Help Instructions

For help at any time, reply:

HELP

You will receive the following message:

"This is a personal SMS assistant operated by Sarthak Agrawal. It sends reminders, notifications, and assistant responses after you opt in. Reply STOP to opt out at any time."

User Scope

This service is currently intended for a single user (the owner) and is not offered to the general public. Messages are only sent following explicit opt-in and user-initiated interaction.

Privacy

Message content and data are used solely to operate this personal SMS assistant.

No data is sold or shared with third parties except as required to deliver SMS messages through messaging providers.

Contact

If you have questions about this service or messaging consent, reply HELP via SMS.
